✦ 企業通知
Q1
＿＿ that the budget had been finalized, the department heads proceeded to assign tasks to their teams.
【中譯】由於預算已確定，各部門主管開始為各自的團隊分派任務。
A
Having confirmed
B
Confirmed
C
To confirm
D
Confirming
解題關鍵
分詞構句中，完成式分詞 Having + pp 表示「先於主句動作完成的主動行為」；確認預算在前，分派任務在後，且主詞 department heads 主動確認，故選 Having confirmed。
陷阱提示
Confirmed 為過去分詞，表被動，主詞不是被確認的對象；Confirming 表同步，時間順序不符。需注意完成式分詞 Having + pp 與一般現在分詞 -ing 的差異。
文法補充
完成式分詞構句 Having + p.p. 表主動且先於主句：Having finished the report, she left the office.
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
Having confirmedv-ing(完成式分詞)/ˈhævɪŋ kənˈfɜːrmd/（已）確認後完成式分詞構句，表示主動且動作先於主句
Confirmedv-pp(過去分詞)/kənˈfɜːrmd/被確認過去分詞構句表被動，但主詞 department heads 是主動確認者，故不適用
To confirmto-inf/tə kənˈfɜːrm/為了確認不定詞表目的，但後句 proceeded 說明動作已在確認後發生，非目的關係
Confirmingv-ing(現在分詞)/kənˈfɜːrmɪŋ/正在確認現在分詞表同時進行或伴隨，但確認預算發生於分派任務之前，時間順序不符

文章 1 · notice
Position: Senior Content Strategist
Department: Marketing
Reporting to: Head of Digital Marketing

We are seeking a creative and data-driven Senior Content Strategist to join our expanding marketing team. The successful candidate will be responsible for developing and executing content strategies across multiple digital platforms, managing a team of two content writers, and collaborating with the SEO and design teams.

Requirements:
- Minimum 5 years of experience in content marketing or a related field
- Proven track record of increasing organic traffic and audience engagement
- Proficiency in content management systems (CMS) and analytics tools
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills

This is a full-time, on-site position based at our headquarters in Taipei. A competitive compensation package, including performance bonuses and health insurance, is offered. Applications close on June 15th.
文章 2 · email
Subject: Application — Senior Content Strategist

Dear Hiring Manager,

I am writing to express my interest in the Senior Content Strategist position listed on your company's website. With seven years of experience in content marketing and a demonstrated ability to grow audience engagement across social media and SEO-driven platforms, I believe I am well positioned to contribute to your marketing team.

In my current role at a regional digital agency, I led a team of three writers and increased organic traffic for our key client by 42% over 18 months. I am also proficient in WordPress, HubSpot, and Google Analytics.

Sincerely,
David Lin
