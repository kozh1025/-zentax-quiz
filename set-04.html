Q1
The new software update will be installed ＿＿ all company computers over the weekend to minimize disruption to daily operations.
【中譯】新的軟體更新將在週末 __ 所有公司電腦上安裝，以盡量減少對日常運營的干擾。
A
for
B
by
C
on
D
with
解題關鍵
installed 後接裝置或平台時，英文慣用介系詞 on，如 installed on computers / on devices，故選 on。
陷阱提示
by 常見於被動語態後接執行者，但此處後接 'all company computers' 是安裝的載體而非執行者，不用 by。
文法補充
介系詞搭配：install on + 裝置/平台（固定搭配）
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
forprep/fɔːr/為了；對於used to indicate purpose 表目的
byprep/baɪ/由；在…之前used to indicate agent or deadline 表執行者或截止時間
onprep/ɒn/在…上；於used with surfaces or devices 用於表示裝置或平台
withprep/wɪð/與；用used to indicate accompaniment or instrument 表伴隨或工具
Q2
The audit committee recommended that every acquisition proposal ＿＿ thoroughly evaluated by an independent panel before final approval.
【中譯】審計委員會建議每一項收購提案在最終批准前都應由獨立小組進行徹底__。
A
be
B
is
C
was
D
being
解題關鍵
建議/要求類動詞（recommend, suggest, insist, propose）後的 that 子句使用虛擬語氣（原形動詞）；被動式為 S + be + p.p.
陷阱提示
口語中有人用 'is'（直述語氣），但正式書面英語及考試標準答案為虛擬語氣 'be'
文法補充
虛擬語氣（Subjunctive Mood）：recommend/suggest/propose/insist + that + S + V原形；被動結構 → that + S + be + p.p.
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
bev. (subjunctive)/biː/（虛擬語氣原形，被動）recommend that + S + be + p.p. ✓
isv. (indicative)/ɪz/（直述語氣現在式）建議類動詞後的 that 子句用虛擬語氣，不用直述語氣
wasv. (past)/wɒz/（過去式）虛擬語氣用原形動詞，非過去式 was
beingv. (progressive)/ˈbiːɪŋ/（現在進行式）虛擬語氣不用 being；原形 be 才正確
Q3
All employees are required to submit their timesheets by 5 p.m. on Friday, ＿＿ their department or location.
【中譯】所有員工須在週五下午五點前提交工時表，__其部門或地點。
A
in spite of
B
due to
C
regardless of
D
according to
解題關鍵
regardless of 表示『無論...如何，皆不例外』，是公司規定公告中排除所有例外的首選介詞；in spite of 強調讓步（雖然如此）；due to 強調原因；according to 強調依據來源。
陷阱提示
in spite of（雖然如此，但）與 regardless of（無論如何）語義相近，但 regardless of 不帶讓步意味，僅強調排除所有例外條件，更適合公司規定用語。
單字/片語詞性音標主要意思搭配用法
in spite ofprep/ɪn spaɪt/儘管（讓步）in spite of the difficulty
due toprep/djuː tuː/由於（原因）due to unforeseen circumstances
regardless ofprep/rɪˈɡɑːrdlɪs/不論、無論（排除例外）regardless of rank / location ✓
according toprep/əˈkɔːrdɪŋ/根據according to the policy

文章 1 · memo
MEMORANDUM

TO: All Employees
FROM: Sandra Yeh, HR Director
DATE: May 15
SUBJECT: New Flextime Policy — Effective Q3

We are pleased to announce that a flexible working hours (flextime) program will take effect beginning July 1. Under this policy, eligible employees may adjust their daily start and end times within a two-hour window, provided that core hours (10:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m.) are maintained.

To participate, employees must submit a Flextime Request Form to their direct supervisors no later than June 10. Supervisors are expected to review and approve or deny requests within five business days of receipt. Approved schedules must remain consistent for a minimum of one quarter before any modification may be requested.

This program currently applies to full-time employees in the Operations and Marketing divisions. Employees with client-facing roles involving fixed schedules are not eligible. Questions may be directed to the HR department at hr@company.com.
文章 2 · email
FROM: David Lam, Marketing Division Manager
TO: Sandra Yeh, HR Director
DATE: May 17
SUBJECT: RE: New Flextime Policy — Effective Q3

Dear Sandra,

Thank you for the memo regarding the upcoming flextime program. The team is generally enthusiastic, and I appreciate the effort HR has put into designing this initiative.

However, I have a couple of clarifying questions before I circulate the information to my staff. First, the memo states that requests must be submitted by June 10, but it does not specify whether this deadline applies to all shifts or only to the standard daytime schedule. Could you confirm this point?

Second, I noticed the policy covers the Operations and Marketing divisions. Are contract staff embedded within the Marketing division also covered, or is eligibility limited to direct full-time hires only?

I look forward to your response so I can relay accurate information to my team.

Best regards,
David Lam
